System Requirements
Daily Operation:
  • Operation Time: 9 am - 5 pm (Monday to Friday)
  • Lunch Break: 45 mins (dynamic, but usually 12:30 pm - 1:15 pm)
  • Dispensary Shifts: 2-hour sections (9-11 am, 11-1 pm, 1-3 pm, 3-5 pm)
  • One pharmacist must be in the dispensary at any time
  • Each pharmacist should ideally have only one dispensary shift per day unless backfilled
Ward Areas:
  • EAU: Minimum 1 pharmacist, ideally 2
  • Surgery: Minimum 1 pharmacist, ideally 2
  • ITU: Ideally 1 pharmacist (not mandatory, requires training)
  • Care of the Elderly: Minimum 1 pharmacist, ideally 2
  • Medicine: Minimum 4 pharmacists, ideally 6
Warfarin Clinics:
  • PHAR2PSP: Tuesday 1 pm - 3 pm (primary clinic)
  • Other clinics are covered as needed
